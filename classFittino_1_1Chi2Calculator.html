Detailed Description

Calculates the chi2 from observables and correlations between the observables.

The observables and correlations are represented by the two helper classes Measurement and Correlation.

The following output quantities are provided:

  • Chi2 The total chi2 neglecting correlations between the observables.
  • CorrelatedChi2 The total chi2 taking correlations into account.
  • For each observable with name [Name] the following quantities:
    • Measurement_[Name] The measured value.
    • Deviation_[Name] The deviation of the prediction from the measurement. If the observable is a lower (upper) limit and the prediction is larger (smaller) than the measurement the deviation is equal to 0. Otherwise it is the difference ( prediction - measurement ).
    • Pull_[Name] The pull, i.e the deviation divided by the total uncertainty.
    • Chi2_[Name] The chi2, i.e. the square of the pull.
